About

For my project, I considered the relationship between the alphabet and certain images as a modern day form of communication.
In order to do this, I created animated "letter-scapes" to reveal different perspectives of landscapes or cities as the
user hovers over the word. First, the viewer will focus on the letter as it is normally read, and then the image will appear on a
plane of the other side of the letter. I am interested in the idea that certain letters can inhabit an endless
amount of subjects, and I like the element of surprise that comes with showcasing a different kind of alphabet.
