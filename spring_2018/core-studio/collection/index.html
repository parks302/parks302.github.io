Boston Fern 01 Boston Fern

The Boston Fern is paired with our Olmsted Planter, an American-made ceramic pot measuring 5˝ tall and 4˝ wide. Your plant height may vary slightly. It comes potted in our potting mix to increase plant health, longevity, and growth. The Olmsted does not have a drainage hole. It has a layer of porous lava rocks lining the bottom to ensure proper drainage.
